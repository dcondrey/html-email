Summary

Welcome to your Bouqs Co. business stationary template. You can use this template in combination with your Bouqs.com email account which you should already have.

To get started, simply select this text and replace it with whatever you want.

Read the step by step instructions below to send your email once you've finished preparing it.

Step-by-Step Instructions

Make sure your email is fully prepared before exporting it to Gmail because you will not be able to modify it in the web-mail client. It may be possible to edit in Gmail but attempting to do so, could easily cause the layout to become corrupted

  1. You should save and archive your messages in case you ever want to modify them later on.
    • To save the template for modification later, select 'File > Save As' in your browser.
  2. Click twice outside of the editable region of the document, to bring focus back to the full document. Clicking on the 'The Bouqs Co' image above will suffice.
  3. Highlight the entire document.
    • CMD + A, CMD + C with the keyboard
    • Edit > Select All, Edit > Copy with the menu options
    • Right-Click > Select All, Right-Click > Copy with the mouse
  4. At this point the entire page should appear highlighted. If not then you did not successfully remove your focus from the edit region and should return to step 2.
  5. Open Gmail and select 'New Email'
  6. Paste the contents of your clipboard into a New Email.
    • CMD + P with the keyboard
    • Edit > Paste with the menu options
    • Right-Click > Paste with the mouse

That's it!
